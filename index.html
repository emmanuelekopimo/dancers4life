Classes categories

Hip-Hop Dance

Hip hop dance, rooted in hip hop culture, consists of diverse street dance styles performed to hip hop music. It emerged in the 1970s and gained popularity through U.S. dance crews.

Salsa Dance

Salsa, a Latin dance linked to the music genre of the same name, gained popularity in the United States in the 1960s, particularly in New York City.

Ballroom Dance

Ballroom dance is a collaborative form of dance where couples synchronize step patterns to rhythmically express the nuances of music.

Line Dance

A line dance is a choreographed dance where individuals follow a repeated sequence of steps, arranged in lines or rows facing the same direction or occasionally each other.
